This is one of my favorite poems ever, it's called "Hagamos un trato" (Lets make a deal) by Mario Benedetti. The poem is originally in Spanish, but I wanted to translate it to dedicate it to you so you can understand it

Companion,you know

you can count on me

not just up to two

or up to ten

but count

on me

if you ever notice that I look into your eyes

and a spark of love appears in mine,

dont raise your weapons or think that Im delirious,

despite the spark,

or maybe because it exists,

you can count on me

if other times

you find me

sullen for no reason

do not think 'what a bore'

you can still count

on me

but let's make a deal

I would like to count

on you

it is so nice

to know that you exist

one feels alive

and when I say this

I mean counting

even if just up to two

even if just up to five

not so that you run

eagerly to my aid

but to know

for certain

that you know you can

count on me.
